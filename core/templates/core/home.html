GradeChecker

What is this?

GradeChecker was built to create a rank list of students (which is not officially released) while preserving privacy, and to calculate statistics, such as the median/average grades of a department, your detailed performance, etc. It is also being built as an alternative to Kronos2.0. While Kronos2.0 uses some hardcoded values to calculate the grading curve of a particular subject, this site can automatically calculate using the grades of different students. However, for this to be effective, we need the data of as many students as possible. You can help by adding yourself and/or your friends to this site. Enjoy!

How to use this?

To add someone, you need their roll number and date of birth. Once you have the information, head on to Add a new student to add someone. To view the rank list, head on to View list. To view the grades of a particular student, use the form at the bottom of this page.

How does it work?

There is a ERP portal, StudentPerformanceV2. This website just collects data from the portal and is aggregated to calculate various things.

Your privacy is protected.

The rank list only shows the CGPA and department/course. Therefore, no one will be able to identify you. That's why it is requested you add your data to the site, since the collected data will be useful for all.
